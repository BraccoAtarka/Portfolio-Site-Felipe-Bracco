Multiplayer

an app for tabletop games

Overview

Since everybody wants better in-game experiences and a greater community when it comes to tabletop* games, I created Multiplayer, an app with which users can search for Helpers (and also register as one) to solve issues with rules, playability, and more.

They can also log their games, share their own content within the community, and check in the feed what is happening in the tabletop game universe.

Now let's dive into the story of the app that I developed, aiming to create a solid game community and share the knowledge of games. By the end of it, you'll find a clickable prototype and a walkthrough video of the app. Enjoy!

*tabletop are games that are normally played on a table or other flat surface, such as board games, card games, dice games, miniature wargames, etc.
